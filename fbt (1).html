Fleet & settings

Pulls the live fleet from your FTC worker (/fleetai/fbt-data), enriching make/model/type per vehicle from the object-status feed, and classifies every vehicle automatically. Statutory rate is a flat 20%; councils pay FBT in full (no rebate/exemption cap applied).

Make/model coming through wrong? Overlay Rick’s object-status feed

If the live list shows device codes (e.g. FMC130) or blanks instead of real makes, paste the object-status response here to overwrite make/model by IMEI/asset.

Bulk-add base values from the council register

Paste one vehicle per line as rego,basevalue (e.g. 717702,52000). Matched by registration/asset number.
